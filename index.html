Amy Clarke - UXD Student

I am a new, curious designer and I am still trying to discover all of my abilities. I am unique using minimalistic style with colours, typically soft / pastel. I like to be descriptive through design, the beauty is in the eye of the beholder, and it gives more space for interpretation this way. I’m very easily distracted so my designs take time, I do believe that I work on them until I am happy and satisfied with what I had visioned. I like to keep things simple, to avoid reason for extra distraction. I believe the colours I chose are those of which represent me the most, they’re some of my favourites. The lilac represents euphoria, and the mint represents comfort.
